## 💡 Key Business Insights Discovered

### 🔴 Critical Findings
- **20.6% customer retention** - 80% of new customers never return (major opportunity!)
- **Customer concentration risk** - 933 "Champions" generate 70% of revenue ($6.2M)
- **Market dependency** - UK represents 84.5% of revenue, international untapped
- **Guest customer gap** - Registered customers spend 73% more per transaction

### 🟢 Positive Indicators
- **Low return rate** - Only 1.7% (vs 8-10% industry average)
- **High transaction value** - $532 average order (bulk purchasing pattern)
- **Strong seasonality** - Clear November peak ($1.5M/month)
- **B2B patterns** - Thursday @ 10 AM peak indicates business customers

### 📈 Growth Opportunities
1. **Retention campaigns** - Target first 30 days (convert 80% drop-off)
2. **Guest conversion** - Loyalty program could unlock $1.73M+ additional revenue
3. **Geographic expansion** - International markets show 15.5% untapped potential
4. **Product bundling** - 1,162 frequently co-purchased pairs identified
